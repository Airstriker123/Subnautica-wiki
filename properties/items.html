Items

Discover a wide range of essential items and tools in Subnautica:

  • Survival Knife: A basic cutting tool used for gathering resources and self-defense.
  • Flashlight: Provides illumination in dark environments and can be attached to the player's helmet for hands-free use.
  • Air Bladder: Inflatable device that helps the player reach the surface quickly for oxygen replenishment.
  • Scanner: Allows the player to analyze and collect data on various objects, including flora, fauna, and technology.

How to Craft Them

Learn the crafting recipes for essential items in Subnautica:

  • Survival Knife: Crafted using titanium and silicone rubber. Requires the use of a Fabricator.
  • Flashlight: Created using a battery and glass. The recipe is available in the Fabricator.
  • Air Bladder: Crafted with silicon rubber and bladderfish. The blueprint can be found in the Fabricator.
  • Scanner: Crafted using titanium and a battery. The blueprint is acquired early in the game.

Where to Find Resources

Locate essential resources to gather materials for crafting in Subnautica:

  • Titanium: Found by salvaging metal salvage, limestone outcrops, or by scanning certain fragments.
  • Silicone Rubber: Crafted using creepvine seed clusters, which are obtained by cutting creepvines.
  • Bladderfish: Found in various biomes, particularly near the water's surface. Can be caught using the Survival Knife or the Stasis Rifle.
  • Battery: Created by combining acid mushrooms, copper ore, and ribbon plants.

Blueprints for the Item

Unlock the blueprints required to craft specific items in Subnautica:

  • Survival Knife: The blueprint is available by default at the start of the game.
  • Flashlight: Obtained by scanning the flashlight fragment found in wreckage or in the Aurora's cargo area.
  • Air Bladder: The blueprint can be found in the Lifepod or obtained by scanning bladderfish.
  • Scanner: The blueprint is acquired early in the game by scanning fragments or objects with scannable technology.

What They Are Used For

Learn the purposes and functions of various items in Subnautica:

  • Survival Knife: Used for gathering resources, self-defense against hostile creatures, and opening sealed doors.
  • Flashlight: Provides illumination in dark areas, making it easier to navigate and locate items.
  • Air Bladder: Allows the player to quickly ascend to the water's surface, replenishing oxygen without consuming resources.
  • Scanner: Used to analyze and collect data on various objects, including flora, fauna, and technology. Scanning fragments is crucial for unlocking new blueprints.
